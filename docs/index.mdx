## How ChargeGo works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Register at the ChargeGo web UI or via the API. You'll have access to all user features immediately after sign-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your EV">
    Register your electric vehicle with its brand, model, battery capacity, and connector type (CCS, TYPE2, or CHAdeMO).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a charging station">
    Use the station discovery endpoint to filter available stations by connector type, power output, price per kWh, and your GPS coordinates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book a reservation">
    Reserve a charger for a specific time window. ChargeGo automatically validates connector compatibility and checks for scheduling conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start charging">
    When your reservation time arrives, start the session via the API or UI. ChargeGo tracks energy consumed and calculates the total cost when you end the session.
  </Step>
</Steps>
