ChargeGo is an electric vehicle charging reservation and management platform built for drivers who want control over when and where they charge. Whether you own a single EV or manage a fleet, ChargeGo gives you the tools to find compatible chargers nearby, book a time slot before you arrive, and track your energy consumption and costs session by session.

## What you can do with ChargeGo

ChargeGo covers the full charging workflow from discovery to payment tracking:

- **Discover stations** — search and filter charging stations by connector type, power output, price per kWh, and availability status. Optionally pass your location to see distance from each station.
- **Reserve a charger** — book a specific charger for a time window. ChargeGo validates connector compatibility, checks for overlapping reservations, and confirms the charger is available before accepting your booking.
- **Manage your vehicles** — register one or more EVs with their connector type and battery capacity so ChargeGo can match them to compatible chargers automatically.
- **Track charging sessions** — start a session against your reservation, record your starting battery level, and receive a full breakdown of kWh consumed and total cost when you end the session.
- **Web UI** — everything above is also accessible through a browser-based interface at [charge-go.duckdns.org](https://charge-go.duckdns.org/).

## Try the live demo

The ChargeGo platform is running live at **[https://charge-go.duckdns.org/](https://charge-go.duckdns.org/)**. You can register an account and start making reservations immediately — no installation needed.

<Note>
  Admin users also have access to Swagger UI at [https://charge-go.duckdns.org/swagger-ui.html](https://charge-go.duckdns.org/swagger-ui.html), where every API endpoint is documented and can be exercised interactively.
</Note>
